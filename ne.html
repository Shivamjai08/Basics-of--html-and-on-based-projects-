Craving Something Delicious?

Zomato is an Indian multinational restaurant aggregator and food delivery company. It was founded by Deepinder Goyal and Pankaj Chaddah in 2008. Zomato provides information, menus, and user reviews of restaurants, as well as food delivery options from partner restaurants in more than 1,000 Indian cities.
